Detailed Description

Implements the specific strategy for the Strong Skeleton enemy type.

This class extends EnemyStrategy and defines the unique properties and behaviors for Strong Skeleton enemies, such as their speed, health, damage, animations, and the level they primarily appear in. It follows a Singleton pattern to ensure only one instance of this strategy configuration exists.
