\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implements the specific strategy for the Ghost enemy type. 

This class extends \mbox{\hyperlink{classPaooGame_1_1Strategies_1_1EnemyStrategies_1_1EnemyStrategy}{Enemy\+Strategy}} and defines the unique properties and behaviors for Ghost enemies, such as their speed, health, damage, animations, and the level they primarily appear in. It follows a Singleton pattern to ensure only one instance of this strategy configuration exists.
