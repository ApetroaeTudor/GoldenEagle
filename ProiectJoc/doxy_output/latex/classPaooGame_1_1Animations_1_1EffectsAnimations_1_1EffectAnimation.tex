\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages and renders animations for visual effects in the game. 

This class extends the base \mbox{\hyperlink{classPaooGame_1_1Animations_1_1Animation}{Animation}} class to handle specific types of effects, such as attack explosions. It loads animation frames from a spritesheet and controls their playback.
