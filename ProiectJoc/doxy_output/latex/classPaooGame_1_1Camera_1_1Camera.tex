\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages the game\textquotesingle{}s viewport, including its position (offset) and zoom level (scale). 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPaooGame_1_1Camera_1_1Camera}{Camera}} class is responsible for transforming the game world coordinates into screen coordinates, allowing the player to see different parts of the level and providing zoom effects.
