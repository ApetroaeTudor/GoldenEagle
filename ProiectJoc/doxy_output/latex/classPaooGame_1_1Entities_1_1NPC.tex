\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a Non-\/\+Player Character (\mbox{\hyperlink{classPaooGame_1_1Entities_1_1NPC}{NPC}}) in the game, specifically a Goblin. 

This \mbox{\hyperlink{classPaooGame_1_1Entities_1_1NPC}{NPC}} has two states\+: active (bound) and passive (freed). The player can interact with the \mbox{\hyperlink{classPaooGame_1_1Entities_1_1NPC}{NPC}} to change its state. It uses static animations for its visual representation.
